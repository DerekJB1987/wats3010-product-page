Mission

Our team is passionate about keeping family and friends connected in a safe & convenient one-stop app.
Tag keeps friends and families connected no matter what unexpected distractions life throws in your path and plans.

Tag is a mobile app that can be used to safely track family and friends on your Smartphone similar to locating a new restaurant.

Tag uses Google Maps to show where you are located and where the person you are looking for is located. Locations are only provided if the person has provided permission for you to see their location via connecting through shared phone numbers.
